# NNNN. Title in sentence case

| Field       | Value                                                |
|-------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Status      | Accepted                                             |
| Date        | YYYY-MM-DD                                           |
| Decided by  | Name(s) and/or roles                                 |
| Source      | `.specs/{feature}/...`, `path/to/file.py:Lx-Ly`, etc. |
| Supersedes  | — (or `NNNN`)                                        |

## Context

Describe the situation that forced a choice. What was the problem? What constraints were in play? Keep it short — one to three short paragraphs. The reader should understand *why* a decision was needed without having to dig elsewhere.

## Decision

State the decision in the present tense, declaratively. "We use X." Not "We will consider X." Be specific enough that someone could verify the decision against the code.

If the decision has multiple sub-parts that travel together, list them. Avoid burying important details in prose.

## Consequences

**Positive:**
- What this enables, simplifies, or makes safer.

**Negative:**
- What this costs, complicates, or constrains.
- Known trade-offs accepted with eyes open.

## Alternatives Considered

- **Alternative A** — rejected because …
- **Alternative B** — rejected because …
- **Do nothing / status quo** — rejected because …

(Only include alternatives that were genuinely on the table. Don't pad.)
